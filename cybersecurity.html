Cyber Security

About

Cyber security focuses on the core security and privacy instances of a website. This includes scams, viruses, pass word protection, icloud, and many more! We will explore difference ways to secure our computers including pros and cons of each! Have you ever wondered why and how passwords work? here you will learn the coding behind it

Why should you learn cyber security? Unlike the other modules, you will learn about what security and privacy is. How to create the best password that 70% of people will never be able to inflitrate! How can we code security, a much needed feature in all websites. We will not only make apps and websites but we will also learn the coding behind it. Learning about interfaces and scamming.

Increased knowledge and productivity is one of the many life changing lessons that this course will teach you! Phishing, online scams, fake phone numbers, fake emails, malicious texts, and many more examples you will learn off and keeo a eye on. This will be very important

Future-Proof Career:As the world progresses, IT majors will always be of importance, cyber security can bring a range of oppurtunities including international jobs!
